Ingredients:
 - 200g spaghetti
 - salt
 - 1 clove garlic, chopped
 - 1 (or more) fresh chili pepper, chopped
 - olive oil (50g)
 

Directions:
 1. Cook spaghetti in boiling water with salt (about 5-6 minutes)
 2. Meanwhile, in a pan, brown garlic and pepper in olive oil for a couple of minutes
 3. When spaghetti are ready, put them in the pan with garlic, pepper and oil and stir for a couple of minutes
 4. Serve and eat
